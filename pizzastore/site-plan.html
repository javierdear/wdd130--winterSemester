Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to match clients with the restaurant. Future clients will find the restaurant on the web and know about their story, menu, and locations. It will be an advantage over others' businesses without a presence on the internet.

Audience

The public is made up of people over 18 years of age, who use the Internet to learn about new places and experiences. They shop online, use GPS and check store reviews.

Content

Home page

We are talking about a website for a Pizzeria. People will come to visit this site because they want to know about the pizzas, where is the store, and how they can get a pizza. Also, they will be able to know the history of the business and see through pictures what we do in the restaurant.

I will answer questions such as who they are, how they make pizza, where they are, how to order a pizza, contact, store hours, and social media.

The navbar will contain the links to “Home” to refer to the main section with a gallery on a carrousel. "Our Story" will contain information about who we are. "Menu" where you will be able to access information and images of our preparations, and the link to "Contact us” with the corresponding links to our communication channels.
