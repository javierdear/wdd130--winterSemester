We are Pizzaiolo, and we have... la vera pizza.

We were founded in 1968 by Italian and Spanish partners, a combination that turned Argentine pizza into one of the most recognized in the world. Time has passed, but we continue respecting the method of elaboration, the ingredients, and the raw material to continue being the most traditional pizzeria in Buenos Aires.
